About Zenith UAV

We combine safe, compliant flight operations with research‑grade processing to deliver dependable spatial products. Our workflows are built from experience running scientific UAV campaigns in Australian agriculture and environmental projects.

“Bold visuals, clean data, zero fuss.”

  • Coverage: Based in Brisbane — available across SEQ and beyond.
  • Deliverables: Orthomosaics (GeoTIFF), DSM/DTM, 3D models, thermal maps, annotated reports.
  • Insurance: Public liability available on request.
  • Licensing: CASA‑licensed (RePL < 25 kg).
